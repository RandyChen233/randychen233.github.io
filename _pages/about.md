My name is Randy and I originally come from mainland China. I came to the U.S. at the age of 16, finished [high school](https://www.hammondschool.org/) in Columbia, South Carolina, and earned a B.S. in aerospace engineering and an M.S. in aerospace engineering at [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://aerospace.illinois.edu/). During my graduate study, I worked under the guidance of [Dr. Negar Mehr](https://negarmehr.com/). My research interests include multi-robot trajectory planning and deep visual SLAM. My master's thesis centered on robust deep visual odometry against dynamic entities.

Please refer to my [CV](https://randychen233.github.io/cv/) for more details.
